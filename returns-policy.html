Returns & Refund Policy

30-day money-back guarantee

Last Updated: January 2025

Easy Returns

30-day return policy! If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

1. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in its original condition
  • In the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Accompanied by the original invoice or proof of purchase

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items that have been used, damaged, or altered
  • Items without original packaging or tags
  • Items returned after 30 days
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items damaged due to misuse or negligence
  • Items purchased during clearance sales (unless defective)

3. How to Initiate a Return

To return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Email us at returns@qumont.com or call +91-XXXX-XXXXXX with your order number
  2. Get Authorization: We will provide you with a Return Authorization (RA) number
  3. Package the Item: Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  4. Ship the Item: Send the item to our return address (provided with your RA number)
  5. Track Your Return: Use the tracking number provided by the shipping carrier

4. Return Shipping

Free Returns: We offer free return shipping for items that are defective, damaged, or incorrect items sent by us.

Customer-Paid Returns: For returns due to change of mind or other customer reasons, return shipping charges will be deducted from your refund amount.

We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance for valuable items.

5. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will:

  • Process your refund within 5-7 business days
  • Issue the refund to your original payment method
  • Send you an email confirmation once the refund is processed

Refund Timeline:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • UPI/Wallet: 2-3 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days

6. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for items of equal or greater value. If you wish to exchange an item:

  • Contact us within 30 days of delivery
  • Specify the item you want to exchange for
  • If the new item costs more, you'll pay the difference
  • If the new item costs less, we'll refund the difference

Exchanges are subject to product availability.

7. Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item:

  • Contact us immediately (within 48 hours of delivery)
  • Provide photos of the defect or damage
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • Return shipping will be free of charge

8. Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong item:

  • Contact us immediately
  • Do not use or open the item
  • We will arrange for the correct item to be sent
  • Return shipping for the wrong item will be free

9. Refund Amount

The refund amount will include:

  • Full product price
  • Original shipping charges (if item was defective/incorrect)

The refund amount will NOT include:

  • Return shipping charges (for change of mind returns)
  • Any discounts or promotional codes applied (unless item was defective)

10. Inspection and Quality Check

All returned items undergo a quality inspection. If an item is returned in a condition that doesn't meet our return policy:

  • We may refuse the return
  • We may charge a restocking fee
  • We may return the item to you at your expense

11. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your order:

  • Before Shipment: Full refund within 24 hours
  • After Shipment: Follow the return process above

To cancel an order, contact us immediately with your order number.

12. Warranty Claims

All our products come with a 2-year warranty on finish and manufacturing defects. Warranty claims are separate from returns and will be handled according to our warranty terms.

13. Contact Us

For returns, refunds, or exchanges, please contact us:

  • Email: returns@qumont.com
  • Phone: +91-XXXX-XXXXXX
  • Hours: Monday - Saturday, 9 AM - 6 PM IST
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 24 hours

14. Return Address

Return address will be provided when you initiate a return request. Please do not send returns to our business address without prior authorization.
