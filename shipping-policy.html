Shipping Policy

Fast and reliable delivery to your doorstep

Last Updated: January 2025

Free Shipping

Free shipping on all orders over ₹999! For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping charge of ₹99 applies.

1. Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (Free on orders over ₹999)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (Additional charges apply)
  • Same Day Delivery: Available in select cities (Additional charges apply)

2. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (Monday through Friday, excluding holidays) after we receive your order confirmation and payment verification.

During peak seasons or sales events, processing may take 2-3 business days. You will receive an email notification once your order has been shipped.

3. Shipping Charges

  • Orders above ₹999: FREE shipping
  • Orders below ₹999: ₹99 flat rate
  • Express Shipping: ₹199 additional
  • Same Day Delivery: ₹299 additional (select cities only)

4. Delivery Areas

We currently ship to all major cities and towns across India. For remote locations, delivery may take additional time. We will notify you if your location requires special handling.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • An email confirmation with your tracking number
  • SMS updates on your registered mobile number
  • Real-time tracking information via our website

You can track your order status by logging into your account or using the tracking link provided in your shipping confirmation email.

6. Delivery Timeframes

Delivery timeframes are estimates and begin from the date of shipment, not the date of order. Delivery times may vary due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Holiday seasons
  • Remote delivery locations
  • Customs clearance (if applicable)
  • Force majeure events

7. Delivery Address

Please ensure your delivery address is complete and accurate. We are not responsible for delays or failed deliveries due to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

If you need to change your delivery address after placing an order, please contact us immediately. Changes may be possible if the order has not yet been shipped.

8. Failed Deliveries

If a delivery attempt fails due to:

  • Incorrect or incomplete address
  • Recipient not available
  • Refusal to accept delivery

The package will be returned to us, and you will be responsible for return shipping charges. We will contact you to arrange re-delivery or refund.

9. Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit:

  • Contact us immediately within 48 hours of delivery (or expected delivery date)
  • Provide photos of damaged items and packaging
  • We will investigate and provide a replacement or full refund

Please inspect your package upon delivery and report any issues immediately.

10. International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping may be available in the future. Please check back or contact us for updates.

11. Special Handling

Some products may require special handling or packaging. Additional charges may apply for:

  • Fragile items
  • Large or heavy items
  • Items requiring special packaging

You will be notified of any additional charges before your order is confirmed.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about shipping, please contact us:

  • Email: shipping@qumont.com
  • Phone: +91-XXXX-XXXXXX
  • Hours: Monday - Saturday, 9 AM - 6 PM IST
